Noodle Stirfry

Description

This is a simple noodle dish i make when i need a quick and tasty meal. It has those simple yet powerful elements i love about this "takeaway"-style chinese cooking, it's sweet, it's savory and even a tiny bit tangy. Very easy to make, you basically just prep everything and it cooks in 15 minutes. Feel free to change vegetables to your liking.

Ingredients

  • Fistfull of noodles of your choosing
  • 250 grams of tofu
  • One carrot, julienned
  • 1/3 a bell pepper, julienned
  • Handfull of white Cabbage, julienned
  • Onion, julienned
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Teaspoon of white pepper
  • Cooking oil

    • Sauce:

  • Soy sauce
  • Ricewine vinegar
  • Cooking wine
  • Tablespoon of Sesam-seed oil
  • Sugar
  • MSG

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles as instructed on the packagin it came in. When they are done, rinse them in cold water, to stop them from cooking more than needed. Mix sesam-seed oil into the noodles.
  2. Cut tofu into rectagles, one to two inches in lenght, no thicker than a pinky. Fry them in oil until they for a bit of a crust (optional).
  3. Julienne all of the vegetables. Finely chop garlic.
  4. Heat up oil in the pan. Add garlic, cook until fragrant.
  5. Add vegetables and tofu, stirfry for around 3 minutes of minutes.
  6. Add soysauce, cooking wine, vinegar, sugar, white pepper and MSG. Mix everyhting togehter and continue stirfrying for 3 minutes. Taste to see if sauce is to your liking, adjust if needed.
  7. Add noodles into the pan. Mix everyhing together. Taste to see if it is to your liking and soysauce, cooking vine, sugar etc. if you feel the dish is lacking
  8. Stirfry for a few minutes more, until everything is incorporated and sauces have reduced.
  9. Take pan off the heat and serve.
